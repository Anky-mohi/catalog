DELTA Professional Handheld XRF Analyzer

The DELTA Professional with X-act Count technology is an outstanding value solution, providing excellent performance in speed, limits of detection (LODs), and elemental range. Housed in a rugged body designed for demanding environments, the DELTA Professional analyzer delivers durability and analytical power with lower cost of ownership.

Key Features

  • 4 W Ag, Rh, Au, or Ta anode X-ray tube
  • Silicon drift detector for high sensitivity
  • Operating temperature range of -10°C to 50°C
  • Analytical range from Mg and up for Rh/Ag, and Al and up for Ta/Au
  • Proprietary Olympus X-act Count digital pulse processor
  • Integrated barometer for atmospheric pressure correction
  • Rechargeable Li-ion battery with hot-swap capability
  • 32-bit color QVGA touchscreen display
  • 1 GB microSD data storage (~75,000 readings)
  • USB data transfer

Applications

  • Alloy analysis
  • Mining
  • Soil testing

Specifications

  • Weight: 1.5 kg (3.25 lbs) without battery
  • Dimensions: 260 × 240 × 90 mm (10.25 × 9.5 × 3.5 in.)
  • Processing electronics: 530 MHz CPU with integrated FPU and 128 MB RAM
  • Power: Rechargeable Li-ion battery; Hot-swap maintains analyzer power during battery change
  • Display: 32-bit color QVGA resolution, Blanview transmissive backlit touchscreen; 57 × 73 mm (2.25 × 2.9 in.)
  • Data storage: 1 GB microSD (stores ~75,000 readings)
  • Data transfer: USB
